=============================================
  AutomateFlow — Setup in 3 Steps
=============================================

STEP 1 — ACTIVATE CONTACT FORM (2 min)
  1. Go to formspree.io → sign up free
  2. Click "+ New Form" → name it anything
  3. Copy your Form ID (looks like: xpwzgkrb)
  4. Open index.html in Notepad / TextEdit
  5. Find:    YOUR_FORM_ID
  6. Replace with your actual ID → Save

STEP 2 — HOST YOUR SITE FREE (3 min)
  1. Go to netlify.com → sign up free
  2. Drag and drop this entire folder onto the page
  3. Your site is live at a free .netlify.app URL

STEP 3 — CONNECT YOUR DOMAIN
  1. Buy your domain at namecheap.com (~$11/yr)
  2. Netlify → Site Settings → Domain Management
  3. Add custom domain → follow DNS steps → Done!
=============================================
